International Victory!

As noted above, I broke at least one rule by playing the Reserve Offensives as guaranteed success rather than rolling as usual.

However, this does show how it's possible to win, because I believe the technique is viable: drive the offensive back and get them off the board early. Having the subversion capability was extremely helpful.
