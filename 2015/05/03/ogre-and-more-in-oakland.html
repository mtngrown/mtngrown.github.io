Ogre and more in Oakland

May 3 2015

Got in a couple of SciFi games this weekend at EGO, and what's becoming a usual game of "pirate checkers," this time with 4 people.

The Ogre scenario was Chokepoint Charlie from the new Barbarians at the Gate book. It's not a bad scenario I don't think. I won this game playing defender, but the person I played wasn't as experienced with Ogre/GEV as I am. Also, he miscounted armor by 2 points, which could have been 2 heavy tanks. I may still have won this one anyway had he brought those two extra armor points in, but probably not by as much. I don't think I would have won it against the resident expert. I'd play this scenario again.
