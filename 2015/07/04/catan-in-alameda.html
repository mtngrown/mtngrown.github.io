Catan in Alameda

Jul 4 2015

I seem to spend a fair number of holidays at PL's in Alameda, and of those we seem to usually get a game onto the table. And this is usually either Catan or Dominion. This time it was Catan for a six player game.

This has to be the worst die rolling I've had in Catan yet. Just really, really bad, and I got the Robber right away for many turns. I went over a full round of 6 people without drawing any cards at all, and never really recovered from that. I did come close using Development cards, but not close enough.

Lessons learned: Soldier cards move the robber and must be played to count. Don't ever give up, even when the dice hate you. This game is so well-designed, that it's possible, if one is careful, to stay very competitive.

Note: my placements were otherwise really good this game. I just didn't have anything on a "6" or an "8," which were rolled turn after turn after turn. Oddly, very few "7"s were rolled.
