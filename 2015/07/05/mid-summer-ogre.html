Mid-summer Ogre

Jul 5 2015

At Endgame as usual.

Game 1: Breakthrough against AW, I was attacker. Won with decisive 56 point advantage, really blew AW away on this one.

Game 2: Breakthrough again with AW, this time playing defender. 20 point deficit for me, technical draw for scenario. AW got quite a few units off, but I killed a fair number as well which brought my vp score up a lot.

Game 3: AW and AS for 3 player Breakthrough, huge blunder I missed seeing a mobile howitzer which fired on a stack of 5 gevs. However, AW forgot to move the mobile howitzer, so I killed it next turn. AW managed to slaughter AS forces, part of which drew those forces away from mine. I was able to get 7 units exited and killed a bunch more to almost balance it out. AW won 102 to 99, iirc.

Another game of Nam Diary, played NVA against AW. I won. AW said this game us not unlike other games where it turns into dice fest, as there is no way to out maneuver without losing a round of fire.
