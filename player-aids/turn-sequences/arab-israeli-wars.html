Arab-Israeli Wars Turn Sequence

The sequence lettering is preserved from the Arab-Israeli Wars rule book.

Turn sequence

Briefly outline the situation for both sides.

A. First Player Air Phase

  1. Next turn's air strikes are written down, specifying the attacking units and target hexes.
  2. Remove DOWN markers from previous friendly air phase (OR-I, C.2d); Helicopters move (AR-I, E.2a); DOWN markers placed as desired after all helicopter movement is finished (OR-I, C.2a).
  3. Air strikes written down last turn are executed. "G" class air strike have a range of 12 hexes from the designated attacker (AR-I, C.2a).

B. First Player Fire Phase

  1. "SPOTTED" counters positioned on units belonging to the first player are removed.
  2. Indirect Fire Attacks.
  3. All face-up units belonging to the First Player may be used to execute direct fire attacks. Attacking units are inverted as soon as they attack.
  4. Engineers attack neutral counters.
  5. Conduct morale check on all "DISPERSED" units.

C. First Player Movement Phase

  1. Move all non-infantry.
  2. There is no "2," rules are misnumbered.
  3. Face-up infantry moves, subject to Opportunity Fire, invert if not CAT.
  4. CAT attacks announced and resolved, CAT units inverted.
  5. Place "SPOTTED" markers appropriately.
  6. All inverted units for First Player flipped face up.

D. Second Player Air Phase

E. Second Player Fire Phase

F. Second Player Movement Phase

G. Advance Turn Marker

H. Repeat Phases A-G

Scenario will specify the number of turns in the game.

I. Game End
